So... I stumbled across an old Jim Rohn clip last night.

Now, this isn't meant to be a rah-rah pep talk, but every trader needs the occasional reset.

In the video, Jim breaks success down into four simple questions.

I'll give you the trading version...

4 QUESTIONS

Ask yourself these today:

  • #1 Why?

    Why put in the work?

    Why study, journal, and stay disciplined when it's easier not to?

    If your trading feels like it's going nowhere, your why might be weak. Strengthen it.

  • #2 Why not?

    Why not see how disciplined you can become?

    Why not see how good your trading could get?

    Most traders settle. "Why not?" is what pulls you out of the typical results pile.

  • #3 Why not you?

    Profitable traders aren't superhuman.

    They just built the habits.

    You've seen glimpses of your best trading.

    You're capable.

    So... why not you?

  • #4 Why not now?

    There's never a perfect time.

    Your schedule won't magically clear up.

    Your mindset won't suddenly click.

    The market won't give you a sign.

    You decide.

    Why not make today the day you actually stick to the plan and become the best trader you can be?

A good one to sit with over the weekend... The Speech that will Change Your Life
